Professional Case Studies

A selection of recruitment, talent development and HR initiatives from my professional experience.

Global Recruitment Expansion & Client Growth

Challenge: The organization needed to support growing recruitment demands across multiple clients, industries and geographic markets.

Action: Supported full-cycle recruitment across Canada, India, the Philippines, the United Kingdom and the United States. Coordinated sourcing, screening, interviews, candidate communication and onboarding while managing the needs of more than 40 client accounts.

Result: Contributed to more than 150 hires and helped establish a more consistent recruitment experience across international markets.

Employee Handbook & Policy Standardization

Challenge: Employees and managers needed clearer guidance regarding workplace expectations, procedures and responsibilities.

Action: Developed an Employee Handbook covering workplace conduct, attendance, leave, performance expectations, confidentiality, accountability and other key employment practices.

Result: Created a central resource for employees and managers while improving consistency across HR practices and workplace decisions.

HR Process Improvement & Workflow Automation

Challenge: Manual document handling and inconsistent workflows created delays across recruitment, onboarding and employee administration.

Action: Introduced structured templates, digital tracking systems, electronic signatures and standardized communication processes.

Result: Improved document accuracy, reduced repetitive administrative work and created a more organized HR workflow.

Campus Recruitment Strategy & University Partnerships

Challenge: The organization wanted to strengthen its early-talent pipeline and increase awareness among university students and new graduates.

Action: Coordinated campus recruitment initiatives, supported university outreach, represented the employer at recruitment activities and guided students from initial engagement through the interview process.

Result: Expanded the early-talent pipeline, strengthened the organization's campus presence and supported hiring for student and entry-level opportunities.

Technical Hiring Across Multiple Business Functions

Challenge: Hiring managers required candidates with specialized experience across consulting, enterprise software and supply-chain functions.

Action: Conducted interviews and supported recruitment for junior and senior positions involving NetSuite, Microsoft Business Central, supply chain, consulting and other technical or functional areas.

Result: Delivered more focused candidate assessments and helped hiring managers identify applicants aligned with both technical requirements and workplace expectations.

Co-op & Early-Talent Program Development

Challenge: Students and new graduates needed clearer pathways into the organization and more structure throughout their placements.

Action: Supported a co-op framework involving candidate sourcing, onboarding, employer coordination, student check-ins, performance feedback and placement administration.

Result: Created a more consistent experience for students, educational partners and placement supervisors while supporting early-talent hiring needs.

Recruitment Process Standardization

Challenge: Different roles and hiring stakeholders required a consistent method for reviewing and comparing candidates.

Action: Developed structured screening criteria, candidate summaries, interview questions and evaluation templates tailored to role requirements.

Result: Improved consistency across candidate reviews and gave hiring managers clearer information when making interview and selection decisions.

Interview Training & Candidate Readiness

Challenge: Some candidates entered interviews without a clear understanding of employer expectations or how to communicate their experience effectively.

Action: Facilitated interview preparation workshops covering behavioural questions, professional communication, resume presentation, research and interview etiquette.

Result: Helped candidates approach interviews with greater confidence, preparation and awareness of professional expectations.

Data-Driven Recruitment Dashboard

Challenge: Recruitment information was distributed across multiple documents, making it difficult to monitor active candidates and next steps.

Action: Created structured Excel trackers and dashboard-style reporting to monitor candidate pipelines, interviews, hiring stages, placement status and follow-up actions.

Result: Increased visibility into recruitment activity and supported more organized, timely decisions throughout the hiring process.

Business Development & Market Research

Challenge: The organization needed additional insight into potential markets, client needs and opportunities for business growth.

Action: Conducted market research, reviewed industry trends, identified potential clients and supported the development of recruitment and service proposals.

Result: Provided research that supported business planning, client outreach and the identification of new service opportunities.

Client Acquisition & Outreach Strategy

Challenge: Client outreach required clearer messaging and a more targeted approach to building new business relationships.

Action: Researched prospective clients, developed outreach templates, communicated service offerings and supported relationship-building with employers seeking recruitment assistance.

Result: Strengthened the organization's outreach process and contributed to new client conversations and recruitment partnerships.

IT Onboarding & New-Hire Enablement

Challenge: New employees needed consistent guidance to begin using internal systems, communication tools and workplace processes.

Action: Supported onboarding by facilitating IT training sessions, introducing internal tools and answering new-hire questions during the transition into their roles.

Result: Created a smoother onboarding experience and helped new employees become familiar with essential systems and processes.

Reference Check & Screening Process Redesign

Challenge: Candidate screening and reference checks required consistent questions and clearer documentation.

Action: Used structured screening forms and reference-check templates focused on competencies, previous responsibilities, reliability and role-specific requirements.

Result: Improved the organization and quality of candidate information provided to hiring managers.

Employer Branding & Candidate Engagement

Challenge: Attracting candidates required an engaging employer presence beyond traditional job advertisements.

Action: Supported employer-branding efforts through campus outreach, recruitment messaging, candidate communications, event participation and relationship-building throughout the hiring journey.

Result: Strengthened candidate engagement and presented a clearer, more welcoming representation of the employer during recruitment.
