Case Studies

Real-world HR challenges I have handled — and the impact I made.

Improving Candidate Experience

Situation: Candidates often waited weeks between screening and interviews, causing drop-offs.

Action: Implemented structured communication templates, automated scheduling, and 48-hour update systems.

Result: Time-to-interview decreased by 40%, and candidate satisfaction improved significantly.

Standardizing Onboarding

Situation: New hires received inconsistent onboarding information across departments.

Action: Created a unified onboarding checklist and policy manual aligned with ESA standards.

Result: Improved first-week experience and faster productivity ramp-up for new hires.

Building Policy Frameworks

Situation: Outdated internal policies lacked clear documentation, creating compliance risks.

Action: Reviewed, updated, and implemented policies aligned with ESA and internal procedures.

Result: Reduced compliance concerns and improved communication clarity across teams.
